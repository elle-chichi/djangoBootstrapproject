  • Tips on how to preserve

    To keep your cakes fresh and delicious, store them in an airtight container at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. For longer preservation, refrigerate the cake, especially if it has perishable fillings or frostings, and bring it to room temperature before serving for optimal flavor. If you need to store the cake for an extended period, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap, place it in an airtight container, and freeze it, ensuring it remains moist and flavorful when thawed. read more
